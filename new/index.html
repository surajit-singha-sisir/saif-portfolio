I am Md Saif Uddin, a PhD candidate at The Education University of Hong Kong, where my research centers on microplastic pollution in aquatic ecosystems and its consequences for organism health. I hold two Master’s degrees in Ecology, with a specialization in Marine Benthic Ecology from the prestigious Ocean University of China, and my first Master’s in Aquaculture from Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Agricultural University in Bangladesh.
My research interests are broad, encompassing aquaculture, fish nutrition, marine ecology, and ecotoxicology. I am always open to collaboration and eager to connect with others who share a passion for advancing our understanding of these critical areas.
